Cobbs Bakery

Cobbs are a truely Highland company. In 2002 Taking took over a farmhouse bakery in the Loch Ness-side village of Drumnadrochit and they inherited a lovely range of traditional home-baked cakes which were already proving popular with customers throughout the Highlands of Scotland. All Cobbs cakes are still made in the old traditional manner, never using additives or preservatives.
