Cat’s Pjyamas Espresso

Henry’s Cat’s Pyjamas (CP) Espresso is by far and away our most popular blend. It has been slowly roasted to produce a deep complex taste with a hint of chocolate and slightly sweet undertone.

Our CP blend is ideally suited to prepare milk based drinks such as cappuccino and latte as the flavour really cuts through the milk.

Blow Your Socks Off Espresso

Our Blow Your Socks Off (BYSO) espresso coffee blend has been specially blended and slow roasted to do just that. It is our premium choice and the first blend we created. It’s powerful taste and smooth, lingering espresso edge makes BYSO a coffee for coffee connoisseurs.
